2. APP DESCRIPTION AND PURPOSE
==================================

8 Ball Path Finder is a mobile application designed to assist pool and billiards players by:
- Using artificial intelligence to detect pool balls on a table
- Calculating and displaying trajectory paths for shots
- Providing visual guides to improve aiming and game strategy
- Offering both manual and automatic detection modes

IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER: The trajectory lines and calculations provided by the App are mathematical approximations based on ball positions, table boundaries, and physics calculations. Actual ball paths may vary due to ball spin, surface friction, table imperfections, detection precision, and other real-world physics variables. The App is intended as a practice and learning tool, not a guarantee of shot accuracy.
