1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to 8 Ball Path Finder ("we," "our," or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. By using 8 Ball Path Finder, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

8 Ball Path Finder is a pool/billiards assistance application that uses advanced AI technology to detect pool balls and calculate trajectory paths to help improve your game.

- Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and basic profile information (name, profile picture) through Firebase Authentication.

- Technical Data: App performance metrics, crash reports, and error logs
- Advertising ID: Google Advertising ID for personalized ads (if you haven't opted out)

2.3 SCREEN CAPTURE DATA
------------------------------------
- The App uses screen capture technology to detect pool balls in real-time
- Screen captures are processed LOCALLY on your device only
- Screen captures are NOT uploaded to any server
- Screen captures are NOT stored permanently
- All image processing happens in real-time and images are discarded immediately after processing

2.4 INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT
------------------------------------
- We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
- We do NOT access your location data
- We do NOT record audio or video beyond temporary screen capture for ball detection
- We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties
